Common Questions

Before you arrive

What is the minimum stay?

Two nights minimum. Many guests choose to stay a week or longer — the land tends to make it hard to leave. Extended stay discounts available on request.

What about food?

Guests have access to a shared kitchen and are welcome to use it as their own.

Can I add healing sessions?

Yes. Private energy healing sessions and Vedic astrology readings are available as add-ons. Let us know when you inquire and we will build your stay around what you most need.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund with 2 weeks notice. Any cancellations after that become a credit toward a future stay — your investment stays with the land.

Is Lotus Temple a sober space?

Yes. Lotus Temple is sober land. We ask that all guests honor this — no marijuana, tobacco, or alcohol on the property. This is part of what keeps the frequency of the land clear and available for deep healing work.

Is this pet or child friendly?

The sanctuary holds a delicate frequency and we ask guests to inquire about children and pets on a case-by-case basis. We want every guest to have the experience they came for.

How do I get here?

Lotus Temple is located near Ashland, Oregon. Detailed arrival instructions are shared upon booking. The Rogue Valley airport (MFR) is nearest. Portland is accessible for those traveling from further.
